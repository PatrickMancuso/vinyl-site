How to Get a Spotify Embed Link

Step 1: Open the Share Menu

Right-click the album or episode in Spotify, hover over “Share,” then select “Embed album” or “Embed song.”

Step 2: Copy the Embed Link

A popup will appear—click the green “Copy” button to copy the embed link.

Step 3: Find the Embed URL

When pasted the embed link will look like this
<iframe src="https://open.spotify.com/embed/album/xyz123"...>
This can be alot so don't panic, this is how it should look
This is the URL you’ll paste into the Spotify Embed field on the form.
