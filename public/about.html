Our Mission

Vinyl Vault is a digital sanctuary for crate diggers, vinyl heads, music nerds, and nostalgic souls. Our goal is to create a clean, immersive space to browse, organize, and share vinyl records — with the style of a classic record store and the magic of modern web design.

How It Started

Born out of a love for album art and analog culture, Vinyl Vault began as a weekend project and quickly grew into a passion. It's inspired by long afternoons spent digging through record bins, the warmth of needle-to-groove, and the joy of discovering music visually.

Key Features

  • 🎧 Listening Station – Explore music interactively
  • 🎲 Shuffle Randomizer – Discover hidden gems
  • 🗺️ Music Map – See where your albums came from
  • 📊 Stats & Charts – Visualize your collection
  • 📝 New Album Upload – Add and tag records

Who's Behind It
